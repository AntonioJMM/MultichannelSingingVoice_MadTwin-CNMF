Multichannel Singing Voice Separation
by Deep Neural Network
Informed DOA Constrained CNMF


This page contains separation results for article "Multichannel Singing Voice Separation by Deep Neural Network Informed DOA Constrained CNMF" submitted to the 28th European Signal Processing Conference, EUSIPCO 2020.

Authors: Antonio J. Munoz-Montoro, Julio J. Carabias-Orti, Archontis Politis and Konstantinos Drossos

Datasets

The experimental evaluation was carried out on the Demixing Secret Dataset (DSD100). A subset of 50 mixtures has been used for the training process together with their corresponding individual sources, and another 50 mixtures for the evaluation.
